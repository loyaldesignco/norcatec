Quality Assurance

NorcaTec is certified to AS9100D/ISO 9001:2015 and registered in compliance with the international traffic in arms regulations (ITAR). These certifications offer our customers a globally recognized standard of quality assurance and our commitment to quality and continuous improvement.

Our Quality Objectives are:

  • On-Time Delivery
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Mitigate vendor rejections
  • Mitigate customer rejections

Quality Policy

NorcaTec provides aerospace, electronic and ground support products and services to military and defense related customers worldwide to meet or exceed their requirements through continuous improvement of process and product.

In support of this policy, we are committed to continually improving the effectiveness and efficiency of our processes and of our Quality Management System to ensure an adequate framework for the establishment and review of the Quality Objectives.

NorcaTec understands our customer needs and through our quality assurance processes, NorcaTec ensures we deliver what we promise with focus on quality. Top management is committed to communicate the organization’s strategy and policies clearly to staff, ensuring that all employees understand and meet the requirements of the NorcaTec quality system.
