NORCATEC TO SHOWCASE CUTTING-EDGE DEFENSE TECHNOLOGIES AT EGYPT DEFENSE EXPO

Garden City, NY, December 3, 2023 – NorcaTec LLC, a leading provider of aftermarket parts and services, is set to participate in the highly anticipated Egypt Defense Expo (EDEX) 2023.  This prestigious event will take place at the Egypt International Exhibition Centre in Cairo from 4-7 December 2023, bringing together key players in the defense industry to showcase the latest technology advancements.

NorcaTec is excited to present its material supply chain and technology insertion solutions that exemplify the company's commitment to the support and sustainment of strategic Military assets around the globe.  As a prominent player in the military parts aftermarket sector, NorcaTec will showcase a diverse range of products and services designed to meet the evolving needs of modern defense forces.

Key Highlights of NorcaTec’s Participation at EDEX 2023:

  • Aircraft Components: Offering a wide range of components and accessories to support primary propulsion engines, secondary power systems, aerostructures, landing gear, and other hydraulic and electromechanical systems for both fixed wing and rotary wing aircraft.
  • Armored Vehicle Suspensions: Representing industry partners specializing in replacement and upgrade components for suspensions including axle systems, shock absorption, torsion bars, road arms and wheels, track, track pads, and track tensioners.
  • Armored Vehicle Drivetrains: Providing components to maintain engines, transmissions, gear cases, final drives, sprockets, and braking systems.
  • Advanced Night Vision: Offering state of the art thermal imaging solutions for land systems.
  • Ground Support Equipment: Supporting ground power units, engine and transmission test stands, accessory test benches, and other equipment and tooling for MRO.
  • Supply Chain Solutions: Providing tailored supply chain solutions, including inventory management and just-in-time delivery services, for DCS and FMS.
  • Kitting: Collaborating with end users to design custom kits to meet MRO and upgrade needs.
  • Technical Support: Offering technical consultations, support, and expert advice.

NorcaTec is honored to be a part of EDEX2023 and looks forward to contributing to the advancement and deployment of defense technology.  The company's participation underscores its dedication to supporting the defense community with innovative solutions that address the challenges of the modern security landscape.

Visit NorcaTec at Booth H2-E44 during EDEX2023 to experience firsthand the latest in defense technology and explore potential collaborations.  For media inquiries or to schedule interviews, please contact Tom Duncan at duncan@norcatec.com.

NorcaTec Further Expands T53 OEM Exclusive Distribution Portfolio

GARDEN CITY, NY, USA – March 4, 2019 – NorcaTec announced today that it has been selected by Honeywell as their Exclusive Distributor of OEM spare parts for the T53 turboshaft engine worldwide, excluding Japan. This agreement expands on its current relationship with Honeywell which began in 2014. NorcaTec will now provide the full range of aftermarket support for Spare Parts, Accessories/LRU’s, Components and Services under this multiyear agreement for T53.

“We are very pleased to be selected by Honeywell as their T53 Master Distributor to provide an integrated aftermarket approach to the global market” said Sam Gorman, President and CEO of NorcaTec. “The collaborative relationship we have developed with Honeywell has made it possible for us to develop a material supply chain solution to enable material availability and optimized customer responsiveness.”

NorcaTec is a leading provider of supply chain and logistics solutions to operators of US-manufactured military equipment throughout the world. Its capabilities include manufacturing build-to-print items, distribution of original equipment manufacturer (OEM) parts, component repair management, parts trading and brokering, and program management. NorcaTec’s prior success distributing Pratt & Whitney and Honeywell engine parts set the stage for this new expanded agreement. “NorcaTec ensures that the customer benefits from the value of our comprehensive offering, our flexibility, and our overall commitment to supporting the market and our customer’s end mission”, commented Executive Vice President, Tom Duncan.

About NorcaTec

Founded in 1946, NorcaTec is dedicated to providing spare parts, logistics and material supply chain support for US-manufactured military fixed and rotary-wing aircraft to operators and maintenance service providers worldwide. As an authorized distributor for OEMs, NorcaTec aims to fulfill its customer’s requirements on time with the highest quality products. NorcaTec also supplies a wide range of spares for naval equipment and armored land vehicles focusing on obsolete and hard to procure items. NorcaTec is a registered small business.
