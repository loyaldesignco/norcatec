Electronics

Our goal is to assist our global customers with procurement and materials management for all types of electronic components. The components we supply are utilized in commercial, industrial, defense, avionic and space applications. Our extensive knowledge and experience in the electronic components market allow us to give our customers unrivaled service and support.

Your U.S. Electronics Component Sourcing Center

  • NorcaTec’s ELTRONICS Division has over 50 years of combined work experience in finding electronic and electro-mechanical components.
  • Global supplier base covers full range of parts from commercial through military and space levels.
  • Components include active and passive components, switches, displays, connectors, circuit breakers, computer products and more.
  • Expertise in providing high reliability components, supplied with various levels of testing and certification as customers require.
  • We pride ourselves in rapid response. Eltronics has the expertise to find the stock you need including obsolete and allocated parts.
  • Aggressive pricing.
  • Current production, forecast and prototype orders accepted.
