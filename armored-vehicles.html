Armored Vehicles

Our Armored Vehicles division has supported Army equipment with superior quality and timely delivery for over 6 decades. Our capabilities continue to expand to meet the critical demands of our cherished customers. Our large library of technical data and manuals along with our dedicated staff enable us to be efficient, prompt and competitive with our proposals.

We have generated an enormous amount of production toolings, such as forging dies, casting patterns, machining fixtures and inspection fixtures. We keep a very large inventory of parts which are utilized for our ongoing production of assemblies as well as giving us the ability to supply parts to our customers with a very short delivery time.

Our capabilities include:

  • Machining of Mechanical Parts – Casting & Forgings, Wrought Material
  • Sheet Metal Fabrication – Ammunition Racks, Boxes, Brackets, Fenders, Panels, Supports
  • Electrical & Electro-Mechanical Assembly – Cable Assemblies, Controls, Control Boxes, Elevation Drives, Harnesses, Power Supplies, Slip Rings, Turrets
  • Hydraulic Components – Accumulators, Cylinders, Elevating Mechanisms, Gunner’s Control Assemblies, Hydraulic Motors, Hydraulic Power Pack Assemblies, Manifolds, Pumps, Risers, Valves
  • Suspension System – Idler Wheels, Road Wheels, Suspension Arm Assemblies, Shock Absorbers, Sprockets, Torsion Bars, Track Adjuster Assemblies, Track Pads, Track Shoes
  • Power Transmission Drives – Gear Boxes, Gears, Final Drives, Transfer Cases, Yokes
