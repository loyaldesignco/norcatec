Naval

NorcaTec supplies a wide range of spare parts for Naval Equipment with specific focus on obsolete and hard to procure items. We support a worldwide customer base and supply the complete range of products to support your end equipment. This is a growing field for us and we look forward to showing you what we can do for you.

  • Supplying high quality spare parts to support Naval equipment for all types of platforms including:
    • Aircraft Carriers
    • Cruisers
    • Destroyers
    • Fixed and Rotary Wing Aircraft
    • Frigates
    • Submarines
  • Our capabilities include, but are not limited to, the following key areas:
    • Communication Systems
    • Electrical Equipment
    • Engine Parts
    • Life Support Equipment
    • Naval Guns
    • Radar
    • Sonar Systems
