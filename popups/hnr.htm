H & R Parts

H & R Parts Co., a division of NorcaTec, supplies parts and assemblies exclusively to the U.S. Army and Marines. H & R builds electronic, hydraulic and mechanical assemblies which are also sold to the U.S. Army Commands and Depots as well as the Marine Corps. H & R is proud to be recognized by the U.S. Department of Defense as a leader in providing a wide range of products in support of our Armed Forces.

H & R Parts capabilities include:

a. Machining of Mechanical parts - Wrought material, Castings & Forgings.

b. Sheet Metal Fabrication - Fenders, Supports, Brackets, Ammunition Racks, Panels, Control Boxes, etc.

c. Electrical & Electro-Mechanical Assembly - Harnesses, Cable Assembly, Control Boxes, Slip Rings, Panels, Commander's Control Assembly, etc.

d. Hydraulic Components - Hydraulic Motors, Pumps, Valves, Manifolds, Gunner's ;Control Assembly, Riser, Hydraulic Power Pack Assembly, etc.

e. Suspension System- Track Shoes, Road Wheels, Idler Wheels, Suspension Arm Assembly, Torsion Bars, Track Adjuster Assembly, etc.

f. Power Transmission Drives - Traversing Gear Box, Elevation Drive Assembly, Azimuth Drive Assembly, Angle Drive Assembly, Auxiliary Drive, Final Drive Assembly, etc.

For more than 30 years, we have generated an enormous amount of production toolings, such as forging dies, casting patterns, machining fixtures, and inspection fixtures. We keep a very large inventory of parts which are utilized for our ongoing production of assemblies as well as giving us the ability to supply parts to our customers with a very short delivery time.
